Pattern Detection

We detect three powerful SMC/ICT patterns that institutional traders use to identify high-probability entries.

Fair Value Gap

(FVG)

Price gaps created by strong momentum moves. These gaps often act as magnets, with price returning to fill them before continuing the trend.

Order Block

(OB)

Zones where institutional orders were placed before a significant move. Price often returns to these levels for re-accumulation or distribution.

Liquidity Sweep

(Sweep)

When price hunts stop-losses above highs or below lows before reversing. These sweeps trap retail traders and provide fuel for the real move.
